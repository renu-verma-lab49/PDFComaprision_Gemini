Testplan powered by Google Gemini

Testcase ID: 1.1

Testcase Title

  • Add Item

Test Topology

  • Android device with Inventory App installed
  • Cloud datastore

Test Procedure

  1. Launch the Inventory App on the Android device.
  2. Click on the "Add Item" button.
  3. Enter the details of the item, including name, description, category, and author/artist.
  4. Click on the "Save" button.

Pass Criteria

  • The item is added to the cloud datastore.

Glue Definition

  1. Given I am on the "Add Item" screen
    • And I have entered the details of the item
  2. When I click on the "Save" button
    • Then the item is added to the cloud datastore
  3. Then I should see the item in the list of items

Testcase ID: 1.2

Testcase Title

  • Edit Item

Test Topology

  • Android device with Inventory App installed
  • Cloud datastore

Test Procedure

  1. Launch the Inventory App on the Android device.
  2. Click on the item that you want to edit.
  3. Make the necessary changes to the item's details.
  4. Click on the "Save" button.

Pass Criteria

  • The changes to the item are saved to the cloud datastore.

Glue Definition

  1. Given I am on the "Edit Item" screen
    • And I have made changes to the item's details
  2. When I click on the "Save" button
    • Then the changes to the item are saved to the cloud datastore
  3. Then I should see the updated item in the list of items
